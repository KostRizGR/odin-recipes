One of the best greek food

Even though this isn't mousakas, it is still a delicious greek food called "Soutzoukakia"
  • 800g ground beef
  • 2eggs
  • 2 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/4cup of olive oil
  • 5 slices of stale bread soaked in wine
  • salt and freshy ground pepper
  1. Beat the pieces of bread in a food processor. Transfer to a bowl.
  2. In the food processor beat olive oil,garlic,cumin and finely chopped mint. Add to the bowl and add the milk,wine,eggs, the ground beef.
  3. Whisk for 30 seconds to combine well.
  4. It is better to wear disposable gloves when handling the mixture and the raw meat.
  5. Mash the mixture together with your hands, until the bread breaks up completely.
  6. Add a generous amount of salt and pepper.
  7. Mix again with your hands until all of the ingredients are completely combined.
  8. Shape the mixture in to long oval shapes, which is the traditional shape of these meatballs. They should weight about 30-50g each.
  9. Add a generous amount of flour to a container. Add the meatballs and dredge them in the flour.
  10. Place a nonstick pan over medium to high heat.
  11. Add the vegetable oil and let it heat for 1 minute.
  12. Shake off excess flour and add the meatballs to the pan one at a time.
  13. Brown them on both sides for about 5 minutes total. They will cook further on the stove.
  14. When ready,remove from pan and place them on a plate lined with paper towels so they can drain from any excess oil.
  15. Set aside until needed.
